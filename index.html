RULES

The rules of "Rock, Paper, Scissors" are simple and follow a cyclical pattern where each choice beats one and loses to another:

  • Rock
    • Beats: Scissors (crushes them).
    • Loses to: Paper (is covered by it).
  • Paper
    • Beats: Rock (covers it).
    • Loses to: Scissors (is cut by them).
  • Scissors
    • Beats: Paper (cuts it).
    • Loses to: Rock (is crushed by it).

If both players choose the same option, it's a tie.

The game is played in rounds, and the winner of each round scores a point.

When someone scores 5 points, we have a winner!
